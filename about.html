ABOUT

GlobeScholar is a helpful tool for international students, developed by Aadit Manyem, an intended computer science major at the University of Maryland. Aadit saw the challenges faced by his international classmates and wanted to offer a solution to make their transition easier. With his technical background, he curated the idea of GlobeScholar to provide a comprehensive resource for navigating college life. From finding events to managing finances and transportation, GlobeScholar is designed to make the college experience less stressful for international students.
